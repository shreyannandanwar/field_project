HOW TO REACH Kotewada

click to get location of Kotewada Grampanchayat

Kotewada is a Village in Hingna Taluka in Nagpur District of Maharashtra State, India. It belongs to Vidarbh region . It belongs to Nagpur Division . It is located 18 KM towards South from District head quarters Nagpur. 6 KM from Hingna. 794 KM from State capital Mumbai Kotewada Pin code is 441108 and postal head office is Bori (Nagpur).

By Road: Nagpur to Kotewada is approximately 18km via NH 7.

By Rail: Gumgaon Rail Way Station , Khapri Rail Way Station are the very nearby railway stations to Kotewada. Nagpur Junction Rail Way Station (near to Nagpur) , Ajni Rail Way Station (near to Nagpur) are the Rail way stations reachable from near by towns.
